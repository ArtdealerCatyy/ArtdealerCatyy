Fashion Glitches

2000's Fashion

September 7, 2024

These images are old fashion runway models from the 80s or 2000s. The reason why I chose to glitch these images, was to create a blur between vintage fashion and how a lot of fashion has evolved through the years and has changed. To make you remember a memory in the back of your head, the glitch makes the pictures slowly disappear in a blur.
